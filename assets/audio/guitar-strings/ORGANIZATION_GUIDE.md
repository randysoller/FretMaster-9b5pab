## 📝 Naming Convention

### Sharp Notes
- `F#` → `Fs.wav` (F sharp)
- `C#` → `Cs.wav` (C sharp)
- `G#` → `Gs.wav` (G sharp)
- `D#` → `Ds.wav` (D sharp)
- `A#` → `As.wav` (A sharp)

**Rationale:** Some file systems don't allow `#` in filenames, so we use `s` suffix instead.

## 🔧 Sample Preparation Checklist

For each sample WAV file:
- ✅ **Format**: WAV (uncompressed)
- ✅ **Sample Rate**: 44.1 kHz or 48 kHz
- ✅ **Bit Depth**: 16-bit or 24-bit
- ✅ **Channels**: Mono (stereo will be downmixed)
- ✅ **Length**: 1-3 seconds with natural decay
- ✅ **Normalization**: Peak around -3dB to -6dB
- ✅ **Silence**: Trimmed from start/end
- ✅ **Tuning**: Accurately tuned to target frequency
